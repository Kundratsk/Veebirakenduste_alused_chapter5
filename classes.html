Cooking classes

At Yoko's Kitchen we offer hands-on Japanese cooking classes designed for all skill levels. Whether you are a complete beginner or an experienced home cook, our classes guide you step by step through authentic techniques and traditional flavours. Each session focuses on fresh ingredients, balance of taste, and simple
presentation. You will learn knife skills, seasoning techniques, and the cultural background behind each dish. Classes are small to ensure personal guidance and a relaxed atmosphere.By the end of the course, you will be confident recreating classic Japanese meals in your own kitchen.

  1. Italian Pasta Masterclass

  2. Asian Street Food Workshop

  3. French Pastry Basics

  4. Mediterranean Flavours Evening

About

Yoko's Kitchen was founded with a passion for sharing authentic Japanese home cooking. Inspired by family recipes and traditional techniques, our mission is to introduce people to the simplicity and beauty of Japanese cuisine. We believe cooking is not only about food, but also about culture, connection, and creativity. Our classes and workshops create a welcoming space where everyone can explore new flavours and gain confidence in the kitchen. Located in Tallinn, we bring a small piece of Japan to the heart of the city.

Popular Recipes

Yakitori (grilled chicken) Tsukune (minced meat patties) Okonomiyaki (savory pancakes) Mizutaki (chicken stew)
